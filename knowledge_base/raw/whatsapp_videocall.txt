Task: How to make a WhatsApp video call

Step 1: Open WhatsApp on your computer.
Step 2: Click on the contact you want to call from the left side list.
Step 3: Look for the video camera icon at the top right of the chat window.
Step 4: Click the video camera icon.
Step 5: Wait for the other person to pick up.

Common problems:
- If they don't answer, try again after some time.
- If the camera is not working, make sure WhatsApp has permission to use your camera.
