Task: How to use WhatsApp Secret Star Message feature

When to use: When someone wants to send a special starred priority message on WhatsApp.

Steps:
1. Open WhatsApp on your computer.
2. Click on the contact you want to message.
3. Type your message but before sending, press Ctrl + Shift + S.
4. A gold star will appear next to your message box.
5. Now press Enter to send. The message will be marked as priority.

Common problems:
- If the gold star does not appear, make sure you pressed Ctrl + Shift + S correctly.
- This feature only works if the other person also has the latest WhatsApp version.
